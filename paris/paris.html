Paris

  • Eiffel Tower

    The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France. It is famous for its iconic design and as a symbol of France and Paris. The tower was built in 1889 as a temporary installation for the World's Fair.

  • Louvre Museum

    The Louvre Museum is located in Paris, France, and is one of the world's most famous art museums. The museum houses an extensive collection of art and artifacts from a wide range of time periods and cultures, including the Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci and the Venus de Milo. The Louvre's iconic glass pyramid entrance and stunning architecture are also major attractions for visitors.

  • Arc of Triumph

    The Arc de Triomphe is located in Paris, France, at the western end of the Champs-Élysées avenue. It was built in the early 19th century to commemorate the victories of the French armies and honor those who fought and died for France.
